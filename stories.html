distribution app

Clickable Prototype

The main things to do in the app is log distribution in a specific region to track consumption of products over a period of time.

The app has different users who have different roles. The solution is to have modular sections that would be shown if you had access to view/access it.

The use of cards is all about showing relevant and digestible information at a glance, but when clicked would give more information in a modal or a new page. Other cards expand to show more information.

highlights (work in progress)

News in Africa is distributed. It's not organized, feeds do not follow standards, and a majority of news sites rely on ad money. Stories are mostly politics or sports, with a few lifestyle and tech ones sharing a thing or two. That is my finding that took more than my professional life.

I am addicted to information, especially tech and science news. I built a few apps that would grab news from different sites and display them according to date. But the apps were only worked on localhost.

In 2017, i have decided to push it mainstream and see what challenges i face along the way. Currently this is the first of the many designs i have come up with. It's simple to navigate, simple to read and simple to comment and share.
