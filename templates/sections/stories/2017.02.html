highlights (work in progress)

News in Africa is distributed, not organized, feeds do not follow standards, and a majority of news sites rely on ad money. Stories are mostly politics or sports, with a few lifestyle and tech ones sharing a thing or two.

I am addicted to information, especially tech and science news. I built a few apps that would grab news from different sites and display them according to date. But the apps were only worked on localhost.

In 2017, i decided to push it mainstream and build a system that would aggregate news and information from all over the continent. Currently this is the first of the many designs i have come up with. The main goal of this design is discovery of content and easy consumption of information.

Main function of the mobile design is ease of use and navigation.
